3. Use of Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be recalled by a web server in the domain that placed the cookie.

Specifically, we use:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics collects data such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This information helps us improve our website's functionality and content.
  • Cloudflare Analytics: To enhance website security and performance. Cloudflare may collect anonymized data regarding your usage of our website to protect against malicious activities and optimize content delivery.
  • Microsoft Clarity: To gain insights into user interactions with our website through features like heatmaps and session recordings. Clarity captures data such as mouse movements, clicks, and scrolling behavior to help us understand how users engage with our content.

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies and the processing of data by these third-party services in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

For more information on how to manage cookies, please visit:
